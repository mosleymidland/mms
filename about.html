About Blossom Buffet

Fresh food, family atmosphere, and a passion for great dining

Our Story

Blossom Buffet was born from a simple idea: bring the richness and diversity of Asian cuisine to West Columbia in a way that's approachable, affordable, and fun for everyone. From our very first day, we set out to create a dining experience that feels like a celebration — not just a meal.

Inspired by the beauty of cherry blossoms, our restaurant combines the warmth of a family gathering with the excitement of discovering new flavors. We believe food brings people together, and our eight buffet stations are designed to delight every palate at the table — from adventurous sushi lovers to kids who just want mac and cheese.

Every dish on our buffet is prepared fresh throughout the day by our dedicated kitchen team. We source quality ingredients and take pride in the variety we offer — over 200 items spanning sushi, hibachi, seafood, Chinese-American classics, BBQ, salads, and desserts.
