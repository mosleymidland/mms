JM

Jessica M.

Visited February 2026
★★★★★

The variety is unbelievable! We came for lunch and were blown away by the selection — fresh sushi being rolled right there, a hibachi station, crawfish, mussels, and a full Chinese food bar. My kids loved the mac and cheese and chicken nuggets on the American bar, while I went straight for the sushi. The dessert and fruit bar was a perfect ending. Staff kept our table spotless and drinks filled the entire time. Incredible value for the price.

Visited January 2026
★★★★★

Best buffet in the Columbia area, hands down. The seafood station alone is worth the price — crawfish, mussels, shrimp, and baked salmon, all fresh and well-seasoned. The hibachi grill was making chicken and steak to order, and the sushi rolls were on par with dedicated sushi restaurants. Add in the drinks being included in the price and you really can't beat this place. We'll be regulars.

MC

Marcus C.

Visited November 2025
★★★★★

If you're looking for a buffet with actual quality food, this is it. The General Tso's chicken and sweet and sour chicken were both crispy and flavorful — not soggy like most buffets. Egg rolls were fresh and hot. But the standout for me was the seafood — the crawfish boil was legit southern style and the mussels were perfectly cooked. Carry-out option is great too if you want to bring food home. We got a pound of hibachi and sushi to go. Highly recommend!
